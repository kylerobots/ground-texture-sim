Create a 4x4 homogenous transform matrix from 6 DOF pose information.

Importantly, the angles are intrinsic Euler angles in RPY order.

Parameters
xThe X component of the position, in meters.
yThe Y component of the position, in meters.
zThe Z component of the position, in meters.
rollThe X axis rotation of the orientation, in radians.
pitchThe Y axis rotation of the orientation, in radians.
yawThe Z axis rotation of the orientation, in radians.
Returns
A 4x4 Numpy array of the homogenous transform matrix for this pose.
